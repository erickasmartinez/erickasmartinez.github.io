Churro Cheesecake

A delicious easy to make churro cheesecake for any occasion. I choose this desert because I recently learned how to make it for the holidays.

Ingredients

  • Churro cheesecake filling ingredients
    • 2 packs of cream cheese
    • 1/4 cup of sour cream
    • 1 can of condensed milk
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • Churro layer Ingredients
    • 2 cans crescent roll dough
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Spray non stick cooking spray on a 9x13 inch baking dish, or dish of choice.
  3. Prepare the cinnamon sugar:
    1. In a small mixing bowl, combine the 1/2 cup of granulated sugar with the 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon, and set aside.
  4. Prepare the cheesecake filling:
    1. In a blender mix the creamcheese, condensed milk, sour cream, egg and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  5. Assemble:
    1. Sprinkle half of cinnamon sugar mix onto the pan, or as much as you'd like.
    2. Unroll one can of crescent roll dough and spread evenly on pan.
    3. Spread the cheesecake filling on top of the dough layer and even it out.
    4. Unroll second can of crescent dough and spread gently over the cheese cake fillig.
    5. Top second layer of dough with the rest of the cinnamon sugar mix evenly spinkled.
  6. Bake, cool and serve:
    1. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
    2. Cool and store in fridge for 3 to 4 hours.
    3. Take out of the fridge, cut, and enjoy.
