Churro Cheesecake

A delicious easy to make churro cheesecake for any occasion. I choose this desert because I recently learned how to make it for the holidays.

Ingredients

  • Churro cheesecake filling ingredients
    • 2 packs of cream cheese
    • 1/4 cup of sour cream
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • Churro layer Ingredients
    • 2 cans crescent roll dough
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
