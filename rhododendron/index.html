Pacific Rhododendron

Alternative names:

  1. Coast Rhododendron
  2. Rhododendron macrophyllum
  3. Rhodies

Physical size range:

  1. Usually between 3-15 feet tall
  2. Can grow up to 24 feet tall if kept in shaded areas
  3. Leaves of the plant are 3-8 inches long

Geographic area they are found:

  1. Native to the Pacific Northwest
  2. Found west of the Cascade Mountains
  3. Grows from British Columbia down to Northern California
  4. Can grow from sea level to about 6000 feet

Preferred climate and Habitat:

  1. Thrives in cool and mild climates.
  2. Grows better in dry, open forests and edges
  3. Commonly grown along roadsides, especially in the rainshadow of the Olympic Mountains
