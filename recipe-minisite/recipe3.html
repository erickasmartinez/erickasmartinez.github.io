Oreo Cheesecake Bars

Ingredients

  • Cheesecake filling ingredients
    • 2 packs of cream cheese
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/4 cup sour cream
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 10–12 crushed Oreo cookies
  • Crust & topping ingredients
    • 2 cans crescent roll dough
    • 6–8 crushed Oreo cookies (for topping)
    • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Spray non stick cooking spray on a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Prepare the cheesecake filling:
    • Mix cream cheese, sugar, sour cream, egg, and vanilla until smooth.
    • Fold in crushed Oreo cookies.
  4. Assemble:
    • Press one can of crescent dough into the pan.
    • Spread cheesecake filling evenly.
    • Add second layer of dough on top.
    • Sprinkle crushed Oreos and powdered sugar.
  5. Bake, cool and serve:
    • Bake for 25–30 minutes until golden.
    • Cool, then refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
    • Cut into bars and serve.

Tips

  1. Don’t overmix the batter
    • Overmixing makes the cheesecake dense.
  2. Use full-fat cream cheese
    • Gives a richer texture.
  3. Chill before slicing
    • Helps bars hold shape.
  4. Add chocolate drizzle
    • Enhances flavor and presentation.
