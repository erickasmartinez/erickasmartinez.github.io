Oreo Cheesecake Bars

Ingredients

  • Cheesecake filling ingredients
    • 2 packs of cream cheese
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/4 cup sour cream
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 10–12 crushed Oreo cookies
  • Crust & topping ingredients
    • 2 cans crescent roll dough
    • 6–8 crushed Oreo cookies (for topping)
    • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Spray non stick cooking spray on a 9x13 inch baking dish, or dish of choice.
  3. Prepare the cheesecake filling:
    • In a bowl, mix cream cheese, sugar, sour cream, egg, and vanilla extract until smooth.
    • Fold in the crushed Oreo cookies.
  4. Assemble:
    • Unroll one can of crescent roll dough and press evenly into the bottom of the pan.
    • Spread the Oreo cheesecake filling over the dough.
    • Unroll the second can of dough and place it on top.
    • Sprinkle crushed Oreos and powdered sugar over the top.
  5. Bake, cool and serve:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25–30 minutes until golden brown.
    • Let cool, then refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
    • Cut into bars and serve.

Tips

  1. Don’t overmix the batter
    • Overmixing can make the cheesecake too dense instead of creamy.
  2. Use full-fat cream cheese
    • This gives the best rich and creamy texture.
  3. Chill completely before slicing
    • This helps the bars hold their shape when cut.
  4. Add chocolate drizzle
    • Drizzle melted chocolate on top for extra flavor and decoration.
