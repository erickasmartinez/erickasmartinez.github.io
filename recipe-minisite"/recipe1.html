Churro Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Cheesecake filling ingredients
    • 2 packs of cream cheese
    • 1/4 cup of sour cream
    • 1 can of condensed milk
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • Churro layer ingredients
    • 2 cans crescent roll dough
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Spray non stick cooking spray on a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Prepare the cinnamon sugar:
    • Mix sugar and cinnamon together.
  4. Prepare the cheesecake filling:
    • Blend cream cheese, condensed milk, sour cream, egg, and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Assemble:
    • Sprinkle cinnamon sugar in pan.
    • Layer crescent dough.
    • Add cheesecake filling.
    • Add second dough layer.
    • Top with remaining cinnamon sugar.
  6. Bake, cool and serve:
    • Bake for 25–30 minutes.
    • Chill for 3–4 hours.
    • Cut and serve.

Tips

  1. Soften cream cheese
    • Ensures smooth texture.
  2. Avoid overbaking
    • Keeps cheesecake creamy.
  3. Chill before serving
    • Makes cutting easier.
  4. Add extra cinnamon sugar
    • Enhances flavor.
