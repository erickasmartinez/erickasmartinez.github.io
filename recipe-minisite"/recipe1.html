Churro Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Churro cheesecake filling ingredients
    • 2 packs of cream cheese
    • 1/4 cup of sour cream
    • 1 can of condensed milk
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • Churro layer Ingredients
    • 2 cans crescent roll dough
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Spray non stick cooking spray on a 9x13 inch baking dish, or dish of choice.
  3. Prepare the cinnamon sugar:
    • In a small mixing bowl, combine the 1/2 cup of granulated sugar with the 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon, and set aside.
  4. Prepare the cheesecake filling:
    • In a blender mix the creamcheese, condensed milk, sour cream, egg and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  5. Assemble:
    • Sprinkle half of cinnamon sugar mix onto the pan, or as much as you'd like.
    • Unroll one can of crescent roll dough and spread evenly on pan.
    • Spread the cheesecake filling on top of the dough layer and even it out.
    • Unroll second can of crescent dough and spread gently over the cheese cake fillig.
    • Top second layer of dough with the rest of the cinnamon sugar mix evenly spinkled.
  6. Bake, cool and serve:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
    • Cool and store in fridge for 3 to 4 hours.
    • Take out of the fridge, cut, and enjoy.

Tips

  1. Soften the cream cheese first
    • Allow the cream cheese to reach room temperature before mixing to create a smoother filling.
  2. Avoid overbaking
    • Bake until the top turns lightly golden to keep the cheesecake soft and creamy.
  3. Chill before serving
    • Place the cheesecake in the refrigerator for a few hours so it sets and is easier to cut.
  4. Add extra cinnamon sugar for flavor
    • Sprinkling a little more cinnamon sugar on top can enhance the churro taste.
